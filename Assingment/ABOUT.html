    • My Travel to Islamabad:

    A tour of Islamabad is a refreshing and memorable experience. As the capital city of Pakistan, Islamabad is known for its clean environment, modern infrastructure, and natural beauty.The city offers a perfect blend of urban development and scenic landscapes. Tourists can visit iconic landmarks like Faisal Mosque, Daman-e-Koh, Pakistan Monument, and Rawal Lake.


  • Must-Visit Attraction

  • Islamabad is home to many beautiful and popular attractions that draw visitors from all over the country.
  • One of the most famous landmarks is the Faisal Mosque, known for its unique design and scenic Margalla Hills.
  • Nature lovers can explore Daman-e-Koh, Pir Sohawa, and the Margalla Hills National Park.

    • My Travel to Lahore

    A tour of Lahore is a journey through history, culture, and vibrant city life. As one of Pakistan’s oldest and most culturally rich cities, Lahore offers a wide range of attractions. Tourists can explore historical sites like the Badshahi Mosque, Lahore Fort, and Shalimar Gardens.


  • Must-Visit Attraction

  • Lahore is a city full of rich history, culture, and stunning architecture. One of its most famous attractions is the Badshahi Mosque.
  • Right next to it stands the majestic Lahore Fort, offering a glimpse into the city’s royal past.
  • For history lovers, the Lahore Museum and Minar-e-Pakistan are must-visit spots.

    • My Travel to Skardu

    A tour of Skardu is a dream come true for nature and adventure lovers. Located in Gilgit-Baltistan, Skardu is surrounded by majestic mountains, crystal-clear lakes, and breathtaking valleys. The serene beauty of places like Shangrila Resort, Upper Kachura Lake, and Satpara Lake leaves visitors in awe.


  • Must-Visit Attraction

  • Skardu is filled with stunning natural and historical attractions that make it one of Pakistan’s most beautiful tourist destinations.
  • Skardu offers breathtaking sights like Shangrila Resort, also known as “Heaven on Earth,” and the peaceful Upper Kachura Lake.
  • The Deosai National Park, known as the "Land of Giants," is a high-altitude plateau with unique wildlife and wide-open landscapes.

    • My Travel to Karachi

    A tour of Karachi offers a vibrant mix of culture, history, and coastal beauty. As Pakistan’s largest city and economic hub, Karachi is full of life and diversity. Tourists can visit historical landmarks like Quaid-e-Azam’s Mausoleum, Mohatta Palace, and Frere Hall to explore the city’s rich heritage. The beaches, such as Clifton Beach and Hawksbay, offer a relaxing escape by the Arabian Sea.


  • Must-Visit Attraction

  • Karachi, the largest city in Pakistan, offers a wide range of attractions that reflect its rich history, diverse culture, and coastal charm.
  • One of the most iconic landmarks is the Mazar-e-Quaid, the tomb of the founder of Pakistan, Muhammad Ali Jinnah.
  • Nature lovers can enjoy the beauty of Clifton Beach, Hawksbay, and the peaceful environment of Bagh Ibne Qasim.
